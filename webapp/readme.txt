AUTHORS: Yiwen Luo

DATA: Information of incident details, location, and victim information of individual fatal shooting by an on-duty office in the United States since 2015 to 2020. 

The data is collected from Kaggle, link: https://www.kaggle.com/washingtonpost/police-shootings

STATUS: Users can navigate from the index page to a search page and a map page. On the search page, users can view demographic breakdown and individual case information based on selection of case conditions. On the map page, users can click a US state and view case number by years.

Notes: Give the search page a little time to load when you first open it up.
